To ensure your developer and designer can build this website with 100% uniformity, you need a strict, documented Design System.

The aesthetic you are building is "Elevated Brutalism"—a high-fashion, editorial look that relies on extreme contrast, zero clutter, and the tension between classic Serifs and harsh Helvetica Bold.

Here is your exact, in-depth technical Style Guide.

1. THE CORE PALETTE
Do not use grays for text. Do not use drop shadows. The design relies entirely on stark, flat contrast.

Primary Dark: #000000 (Pure Black)

Primary Light: #FFFFFF (Pure White)

Accent / Alert: #5C0A0A (Deep Oxblood/Burgundy - strictly used for "Sold Out" tags, notification dots, or error states. Never for text).

Disabled / Borders: #111111 (Off-black, used ONLY for subtle hairline grid dividers when pure black is too strong).

2. TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM
This is the most crucial part of your brand. Never deviate from these exact weights and styles.

Primary Typeface: The Elegant Serif
(Suggested: Playfair Display, GT Alpina, or Garamond)

Role: Emotion, luxury, large numbers, and philosophical statements.

Rule: Can be used in Regular or Italic. Often mixed in the same sentence (e.g., "The new Standard"). Never use bold.

Secondary Typeface: The Utility Sans
(Helvetica Bold, or Neue Haas Grotesk Display Bold)

Role: Structure, navigation, product names, UI buttons, actions.

Rule: Mostly used in ALL CAPS. Tightly spaced.

Exact Web Typographic Scale:
Display / Hero Header (H1):

Font: Serif Regular & Italic

Size: 80px (Desktop) / 48px (Mobile)

Line Height: 1.0em (Very tight)

Letter Spacing: -0.02em

Section Header (H2):

Font: Helvetica Bold (ALL CAPS)

Size: 24px

Line Height: 1.2em

Letter Spacing: 0.05em (Slightly tracked out)

Product Title (H3):

Font: Helvetica Bold (ALL CAPS)

Size: 14px

Letter Spacing: 0.02em

Pricing:

Font: Serif Regular

Size: 18px

UI / Navigation / Button Text:

Font: Helvetica Bold (ALL CAPS)

Size: 12px

Letter Spacing: 0.1em (Wide tracking)

Body Copy (Keep minimal):

Font: Helvetica Regular (Sentence case)

Size: 14px

Line Height: 1.4em

3. UI COMPONENTS & GEOMETRY
Everything is sharp. Absolutely NO rounded corners anywhere (border-radius: 0px).

Buttons:

Primary Action (Add to Cart / Checkout):

Background: #000000 (If on white) or #FFFFFF (If on black).

Text: Helvetica Bold, 12px, All Caps. Inverted color to background.

Padding: 16px 24px.

Hover State: Background turns transparent, gains a 1px solid border, text color flips.

Secondary Action (Filters, Read More):

Background: Transparent.

Border: 1px solid #000000.

Text: Helvetica Bold, 12px.

Tags & Badges (e.g., "SOLD OUT"):

Background: Pure Black #000000 OR Deep Burgundy #5C0A0A.

Text: White #FFFFFF, Helvetica Bold, 10px, All Caps.

Padding: 4px 8px.

Placement: Overlapping the top-left corner of a product image.

Forms & Inputs:

No background color (transparent).

Border: Bottom border ONLY (border-bottom: 2px solid #000000).

Text inside: Helvetica Regular, 14px.

4. GRID & LAYOUT RULES
To achieve the "Editorial / Magazine" look, you must treat the web browser like a printed poster.

Grid Lines: Use visible 1px solid hairlines (black on white backgrounds, white on black backgrounds) to divide sections, headers, and footer. (Inspired by the Uniform reference).

Padding & Margins (The "Extremes" Rule):

Rule of Extremes: Elements should either be touching/constrained by a 1px border, OR they should have massive negative space around them (120px to 160px padding). Do not use average/medium spacing.

Overlapping: When displaying product galleries or campaign images, allow images to overlap each other with absolute positioning. Give overlapping images a thin 1px border to separate them from the background.

5. PHOTOGRAPHY TREATMENT
Because your UI is entirely black, white, and text-based, your photography is the only source of texture.

Editorial/Campaign Shots: Should be "flash-heavy," raw, cinematic, or grainy (like the reference images showing the people at the table and the makeup swatches).

Product Cutouts (e-comm grid): Must be placed on pure #FFFFFF backgrounds so they blend seamlessly into the white UI sections.

Borders: Images placed on a black background MUST have a 1px solid #FFFFFF border to create a stark frame. Images placed on white do not need borders unless overlapping.

6. INTERACTION & ANIMATION
The site must feel mechanical, snappy, and deliberate.

No "bouncy" or "smooth" easing.

Hover effects: Instant color inversion (Black to White).

Page transitions: Hard cuts, no fade-ins.

Scroll: Native scroll. Do not hijack the user's scrolling. Let the massive typography reveal itself naturally as they scroll down the stark pages.

Instructions for your Web Developer: "Please implement this exact style guide globally in the CSS/Root variables before building any individual components. Ensure border-radius is globally set to 0, and set up typography variables specifically separating Serif Display from Helvetica UI."
